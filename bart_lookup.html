Translate HORVU Gene IDs to BART Gene IDs

The relationship between the BART Genes and the HORVU Genes has been calculated using their respective locations on the Barley Pseudomolecules 2017. You can use this webpage to translate a list of HORVU Gene IDs to the equivalent BART Gene IDs and you will get a set of links to those BART Gene IDs.

Please note that not every HORVU ID will have an equivalent BART ID, and some HORVU IDs will overlap with more than one BART ID. Also please note this is not a homology search it is simply a comparison of the two sets of genes based on their locations on the same reference genome.
